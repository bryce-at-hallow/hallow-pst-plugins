# Parish Partner Drive — File Reference

This skill is the single source of truth for all Hallow Partner Drive files. When composing
emails or completing action items that involve sharing a resource, identify the right file(s)
below and embed the link with appropriate anchor text.

---

## How to use this skill

## Keyword Trigger Index

Use this index when another skill (e.g. `attention-roadmap-email`) needs to select resources
based on topics discussed in a conversation. Cross-reference phrases below to file numbers,
then pull the matching entries from the catalogue. Select up to 3 files total — prioritize
the ones most directly tied to concrete next steps.

| Bulletin, bulletin announcement, weekly bulletin | #2 |

> **For dependent skills:** Read this index first, match the topics from the call/conversation,

1. **Identify the context** — What is the partner's tier (Basic/Growth/Expansion)? What phase
   are they in (just onboarded, mid-partnership, event planning, etc.)? What's the action item?
2. **Select the right file(s)** from the catalogue below.
3. **Embed the link** using the suggested anchor text, or a natural variation, in the email.
4. When multiple files apply, include all of them — don't pick just one arbitrarily.

## File Catalogue

### 1. Parish Partner Manual
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1aHg2B6Pw-VwMcrAFquEc2haVTZrr_WKRO-aF-RdmRl8/edit?tab=t.0
**Suggested anchor text:** "Parish Partner Manual"
**When to include:**
- First meeting / kickoff email with a new partner
- Any time a partner has questions or confusion about what's included in their partnership
- General onboarding reference covering all features: parish page, prayer challenges, activation events, comp subs, heralds, and more

---

### 2. Bulletin Announcement Template (English + Spanish)
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1bHFd6uhZnA1hVSmKTeHm3I71FuZgXQOaap3j0MUVqqQ/edit?tab=t.0
**Suggested anchor text:** "Bulletin Announcement Template"
**When to include:**
- When a partner is ready to promote Hallow in their weekly bulletin
- Covers two scenarios: (a) introducing Hallow to the parish for the first time, and (b) inviting parishioners to a specific prayer challenge
- Available in both English and Spanish — note this in the email if the parish serves a Spanish-speaking community

---

### 3. Pulpit Announcement Template (English + Spanish)
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1_Q8zN1MtfMh4m_-SFgfPaOErUs9l4KDu1r0hTPNWaWU/edit?tab=t.0
**Suggested anchor text:** "Pulpit Announcement Template"
**When to include:**
- When coaching a parish on having the priest make Mass announcements about Hallow
- Especially recommended at the start of the partnership — ideally the priest reads this every Sunday early on
- Covers both a general Hallow intro and a prayer challenge invitation
- Available in English and Spanish

---

### 4. Priest Script — General Hallow Intro (English)
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ScYzQ2pX8VtlYvyi-wYEo9mSKeteb6yS/edit
**Suggested anchor text:** "Priest Script for Audio Recording"
**When to include:**
- When onboarding the pastor and asking them to record a guided audio prayer intro for the Hallow app
- Should happen early in the partnership as part of priest onboarding
- The recorded audio appears inside the app for parishioners

---

### 5. Hallow Parish Growth Events Guide
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1JPa02EuMKlkrSN09TFk-ckGKjqikgYyukcees2QhZA4/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.y7b7ae23ls3g
**Suggested anchor text:** "Parish Growth Event Guide"
**Tier:** Growth partners only
**When to include:**
- When a Growth partner is beginning to plan their Hallow-led activation event (1-day visit from a Hallow speaker)
- Covers event structure options, volunteer roles (Event Leader, MC, Heralds, Prayer Warriors), and scheduling guidance
- Recommend locking in the date at least 3 months out

---

### 6. Hallow Parish Mission Events Guide
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1zgdUX8OOSC_wksnL43WC7JXcf-o7YgTY3VIql7WujAw/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.y7b7ae23ls3g
**Suggested anchor text:** "Parish Mission Event Guide"
**Tier:** Expansion partners only
**When to include:**
- When an Expansion partner is planning their multi-day parish mission (up to 3 consecutive days with a Hallow speaker)
- Same role structure as the Growth event guide but scoped to the full mission format
- Recommend locking in dates at least 4 months out

---

### 7. Parish Partner Heralds Description
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Dv2KxJsfgymT5OmnauHH2w-I18GKsIvLlzxlnJVa04M/edit?tab=t.0
**Suggested anchor text:** "Herald Roles Overview"
**When to include:**
- When a parish is ready to recruit and organize their Herald volunteer team
- Covers all in-app roles (Parish Page Admin, Small Group Admin, Prayer Warrior, Hallow Advocate) and off-app event support roles
- Pair with the Herald Deep Dive Video (#8) for a complete resource set

### 8. Herald Deep Dive Video (Maddy)
**Link:** https://drive.google.com/file/d/1jL5L-OlT1ZM3ISTN8N982BnhlO0txaxc/view?usp=sharing
**Suggested anchor text:** "Herald Deep Dive Video"
**When to include:**
- When a parish needs more than the written Herald description — Maddy walks through the Herald program in depth
- Pair with the Heralds Description doc (#7) when first setting up the Herald team
- Also useful for a parish that has Heralds but wants to train them more thoroughly

### 9. Complimentary Subscriptions Setup Guide
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1HUmjs8ubspiSCcAMKcL5gY3f5xZ-2tgN4pgD0jVemmI/edit?usp=sharing
**Suggested anchor text:** "Complimentary Subscriptions Guide"
**When to include:**
- When a parish asks how to set up or distribute their comp subscriptions (staff, volunteers, catechists, or students)
- When the Faith Formation Dashboard comes up — covers how admins roster catechists and students, what each role gets access to, and links to FF Dashboard tutorials
- Covers promo code redemption at hallow.com/redeem for parish staff (Growth: 50 subs, Expansion: 75 subs)
- Covers Faith Formation Dashboard setup for catechists and students (Expansion + Growth with FF add-on): rostering steps, teacher resource portal access, student safe environment policy, and the parents small group
- Also useful any time a parish has questions about how someone redeems their subscription or why a staff member can't access certain features

---

### 10. Parishioner Onboarding & Discount Guide
**Link:** https://docs.google.com/document/d/1y9MjdvLQt5byEbmLMzGAr6vydF2nn-e6Q-uNPX3WGxY/edit?usp=sharing
**Suggested anchor text:** "Parishioner Onboarding Guide"
**When to include:**
- Any time a parish asks about the parish discount, how parishioners qualify for it, or how the discount works
- When a parish needs copy to share with parishioners — bulletin, Flocknote, email blast, etc.
- Covers: how to join the parish page for free AND how to redeem the discounted subscription (new subscribers tap "Claim Subscription" on the parish page; existing subscribers cancel first, then claim)
- Designed to be copy-pasted with minimal customization — let the partner know it's plug-and-play

→ Parish Partner Manual (#1), Founding Story Video (#11)

→ Pulpit Template (#3), Priest Script (#4)

→ Bulletin Template (#2), Pulpit Template (#3)

→ Parishioner Onboarding Guide (#10)

→ Complimentary Subscriptions Guide (#9)

→ Suggested Prayer Challenges Spreadsheet (#12)

**Building the Herald team:**
→ Heralds Description (#7), Herald Deep Dive Video (#8)

**Planning activation events:**
→ Growth partners: Growth Events Guide (#5)

**Giving Campaign (Growth + Expansion):**
→ Giving Campaign Overview (#13), One-Pager (#14), Playbook Webinar (#15)
